About the Program

The RVU Summer Internship Program is designed to provide students and recent graduates with hands-on experience in various fields. Our program connects talented individuals with leading organizations to create meaningful learning experiences.

Program Overview

  • Duration: 4-12 weeks (flexible)
  • Multiple domains available (Technology, Management, Design, etc.)
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Competitive stipend/compensation
  • Flexible work arrangements (On-site/Remote)
  • Certificate of completion

Eligibility Criteria

Basic Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in a recognized university or institute
  • Completed at least 2 years of your undergraduate program
  • Minimum CGPA of 6.5 (or equivalent)
  • Proficiency in English (spoken and written)
  • Valid student ID or enrollment certificate

Domain-Specific Requirements

Technology

Knowledge of programming languages, database management, and technical fundamentals required.

Management

Strong analytical skills, communication abilities, and business acumen.

Design

Portfolio of work, design fundamentals, and proficiency in design tools.

Selection Criteria

  • Academic performance and subject knowledge
  • Practical skills and technical abilities
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Motivation and willingness to learn
  • Attendance and commitment

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the internship program? ▼

The internship duration is flexible, ranging from 4 to 12 weeks depending on your availability and the organization's requirements. Most interns complete 8-week programs.

Is the internship paid? ▼

Yes, the internship is paid. The stipend ranges from ₹15,000 to ₹35,000 per month depending on your domain, location, and performance.

Can I do the internship remotely? ▼

Yes, many of our partner organizations offer remote internship positions. You can mention your preference during the application process.

Will I get college credit for this internship? ▼

It depends on your university's policy. We provide official documentation and certificates. Please consult with your college regarding credit transfer eligibility.

What if I don't have prior experience? ▼

We welcome students from all experience levels. Our mentorship program is designed to help you develop skills during the internship. Passion to learn is more important than prior experience.

How do I apply? ▼

Click on the "Apply Now" button in the navigation menu or the home page. Fill out the application form with your details, and our team will review your application within 2 weeks.

What is the selection process? ▼

The selection process includes application review, technical assessment (domain-specific), and interviews with our team and partner organizations. Selected candidates will be notified via email.
